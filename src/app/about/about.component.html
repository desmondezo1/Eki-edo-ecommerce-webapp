About EkiEdo

For the first time, we are going to have a 100% Nigerian farmers mart, grown on Nigerian soil, by Nigerians but not just for Nigerians, this time for the world and Eki Edo is the vehicle that is driving all of these.

Our Mission

Galvanize smallholder farmers and agro-processors towards promoting better sales and increased profits

Make available fresh and hygienic groceries to Nigerian homes, while maintaining unbeatable affordability

Standardize the quality of our local produce to meet and surpass export grade

Our Vision

To become the number one channel for the sourcing and distribution of groceries in Nigeria, using Edo state as a launchpad
